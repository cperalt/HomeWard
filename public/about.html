About Homeward

Homeward's Mission

Homeward was founded to address the issues of homelessness and the affordable housing crisis. Our website was created to lead users to resources and support for individuals and families experiencing housing instability. Additionally, we offer resources for those who are looking to help combat the issues at hand by connecting them to volunteering opportunities. Our mission is to foster a supportive environment where everyone has access to safe, affordable housing and the chance to have a stable life.

Why We Developed Homeward

Our developers at Homeward initially observed the issues of homelessness and the housing crisis in their own community of Charlotte, North Carolina. This firsthand experience fueled our determination to make a meaningful change. We recognized the urgent need for accessible resources and support systems for individuals and families facing housing instability.

Currently there are 653,100 homeless individuals in the U.S.

Out of these individuals, 37,252 are veterans

186,100 of these individuals are apart of families

We believe in building a future where everyone has access to safe, affordable homes and the opportunity to thrive.
